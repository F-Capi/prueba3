Build and train your walking robot pet!

In the world of robots, a "walker" is a special kind of robot that moves around by walking, much like how you and I, or animals like dogs and cats, move around. Instead of using wheels like a car or a skateboard, they use legs to get where they need to go.

We will be making our own pet that is able to walk like an animal and we'll teach it some cool tricks!

Now that our robot pet is already built it's time to train it.

The robot uses two components, the engine and de color sensor. We can make it so depending of the color that our pet sees it moves forward, backwards or stops.

Important: If the engine is moving in one direction, before swithing the direction we should stop the engine first, since it can't move in both ways at the same time.
